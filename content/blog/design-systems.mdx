## The Silent Killer

Your ads aren't failing because of the creative. They are failing because of the context.

When users are doom-scrolling, they aren't looking to buy. They are looking to feel.

### Key Takeaways

* Focus on the narrative, not the product.
* Use colors that disrupt the feed.
* Optimize for mobile-first experiences.
